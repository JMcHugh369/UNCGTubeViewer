Help & FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload a video?

To upload a video, click on the 'Upload' button in the top right corner of the homepage. You will be prompted to select a video file from your device, add a title and description, and then submit it for upload.

How do I subscribe to a channel?

To subscribe to a channel, navigate to the channel's page and click the 'Subscribe' button located beneath the channel name. You will receive updates whenever this channel uploads new content.

How do I manage my subscriptions?

You can manage your subscriptions by clicking on the 'Subscriptions' tab in the main menu. From there, you can view all the channels you're subscribed to, unsubscribe, and enable notifications.

How do I view my watch history?

To view your watch history, click on the 'Watch History' tab in the main menu. You'll see a list of all the videos you've watched, sorted by date and time.

How do I delete a video from my watch history?

To delete a video from your watch history, navigate to the 'Watch History' page. Click on the three dots next to the video you want to remove and select 'Remove from history'.
